Analysis

Dataset selected were people between the age of 18 to 64 who have disability and the estimate indicating this population having poor health by individual states within the United States and Puerto Rico. Based on the results shown in the visualization, disability does not necessarily mean one is in poor health. As an example comparing California and West Virginia, West Virginia has 200k people with disability while California has over 2 million. Yet, the rating for West Virginia (9.1) almost doubles that of California (4.6).
